Lemon Tuna Alfredo

Ingrediants

  • 1 box of noodles (user's choice)
  • 1 cup of alfredo sauce
  • 1 quarter cup lemon juice
  • 1 packet tuna
  • 5 slices of hard salami sliced thin
  • 2 tsp of Tony Chachere's
  • 2 tsp ground pepper

    • Directions

      Begin by bringing a pot of water to a boil.
      Empty the box of noodles into the boiling water and reduce to half heat for 10 minutes (8 minutes for Al Dente)
      Strain water from noodles.
      Heat alfredo sauce to low boil in a saucepan. combine lemon juice, tuna, salami, Tony Chachere's, and pepper into sauce and stir contunously while bringing back to unniform heat.
      Combine noodles and sauce mixture in serving dish and serve while hot.
      Add cheese to taste.

    Choclolate Eclair Cake

    Ingrediants

  • 1 box Graham Cracker
  • 1 box instant pudding
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 box chocolate frosting

    • Directions

      Mix milk and instant pudding in large mixing bowl until smooth, cover and leave out.
      In 9x13 baking pan, layer Graham Crackers.
      Pour half pudding mixture over Graham Crackers.
      Layer additional Graham Crackers over pudding.
      Pour remaining pudding over second layer of crackers
      Add another layer of Graham Crackers over pudding.
      Heat frosting in microwave safe dish by heating for 30 seconds and then stirring
      Pour frosting over Graham Crackers and then smooth surface.
      Refrigerate for 24 hours and serve cold.

    Beef Stew

    Ingrediants

  • 1 lb beef chunks, cut into squares
  • 1 can Cream of Cheese soup
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 lb of brocoli
  • 10 yellow potatoes diced into 1 inch cubes
  • 1 lb mixed vegetibles
  • 2 tbsp Tony Chachere's

    • Directions

      In large greased pan, brown beef on medium heat.
      Add meat to crock pot along with cream of cheese and water. Heat in crock pot on high for two hours.
      Stir in potatoes and reduce to medium heat for six hours or until potatoes are soft.
      Add vegetibles, brocoli and seasoning to taste.
      Keep on medium heat for one hour, and then serve hot.
